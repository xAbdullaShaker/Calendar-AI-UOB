## Key Abbreviations
- **W** — Voluntary withdrawal from a course (no grade penalty).
- **WA/WF** — Mandatory/forced withdrawal (recorded by instructor/registrar).
- **AH** — Anno Hegirae (Hijri year).
- **IELTS / TOEFL** — English proficiency tests accepted for graduate admission.
